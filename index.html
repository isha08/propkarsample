Who We Are

Our Founder's: Helping the mankind

PAROPKAR society was established in 1996 and registered in 1999 by its founder pioneer members Sardar Surjeet Singh Jhass- Chief Manager, Punjab & Sind Bank-Lucknow; Shri K.M. Khan- Executive Engineer (Rural Engineering) and Dr. R. D. Singh - Retd. Director, Horticulture & Food Processing, Uttar Pradesh Govt. Lucknow.The society is registered under Societies Registration Act 1860 & U.P. Societies registration rules 1976 as per registration no. 2850/1998-99 their by renewed after every 5 years.

About

A bit of background

PAROPKAR is a non profit organisation of few like minded people who have taken an oath for bringing change in society and are driven by a single goal; to do our part in making the world a better place to live.PAROPKAR aim's towards the welfare of people and as a social activist we all do our bit without any prejudice to religion, race, Community, Caste or Creed by enforcing, implementing, publicising, promoting and training the needy about objectives viz. Yoga, Hi-Tech Horticulture, Child education-especially girl child.We do it by organising training & development camps along with organising seminars and Yoga Camps for their betterment.We are also exploring the avenues towards various option in Hi-Tech Horticulture (Orchard keeping, Fruits, Flowers, Bee Keeping, Mushroom Culture etc.) for production, training & skill development alongwith other ideas.

Mission.

With PAROPKAR'S mission always in mind, we strive to provide support for developing and integrating various scientifically tested methods in betterment of Health, education, and horticulture. Our team is working each and every day to make a positive impact.

Vision.

Striving towards better Health, Better Life and quality education to women & Girlchild, women empowerment and promoting scientific methods for increasing fruit crop cultvation through Hi-Tech Horticulture and its produce.

Our Special Attention.

At PAROPKAR, we are dedicated to stepping up our special efforts in addressing the issue of empowerment of Women & Girl Child by way of providing Education, better health , hygene and lifestyle. We believe we can facilitate progress in this area. We are always striving to make a differenceand seek your support.
